Process

How a project usually flows

From a message to a finished website

  • 1
    Initial message
    You send a short description of what you need (using the form or Instagram DM). I reply with a few questions, a rough direction and a price range.
  • 2
    Content & references
    You share your text, photos, logo and any sites you like. If something is missing, I help you shape the core sections.
  • 3
    Design & build
    I create a layout, choose colours and typography, and build the site with clean HTML/CSS/JS. You get a preview to review.
  • 4
    Refine & launch
    We adjust details together. When you are happy, I hand over the files or help you connect a domain and go live.
